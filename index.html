hello.

I'm a second year PhD student working in the Centre for Language Evolution at The University of Edinburgh, supervised by Professors Kenny Smith, Simon Kirby, and Fiona Jordan (University of Bristol).

In my research, I look at typological regularities in kinship terminology with a view to understanding how linguistic and cultural pressures encourage particular relatives to be categorised together. I answer questions about how pressures for learnability and informativeness selected for the distribution of kinship terminology we see cross-linguistically using a combination of artificial language experiments and phylogenetic comparative methods.
