hello.

I'm a first year PhD student working in the Centre for Language Evolution at The University of Edinburgh, supervised by Professors Kenny Smith, Simon Kirby, and Fiona Jordan (University of Bristol).

In my research, I'm looking at the evolution of kinship terminology and how linguistic and cultural pressures encourage particular kin types to be categorised together. I hope to answer questions about how pressures for learnability and informativeness selected for the distribution of kinship terminology we see cross-linguistically using a combination of artificial language experiments and phylogenetic comparative methods.
